Garfield is a lazy, orange tabby cat created by Jim Davis. He first appeared in the comic strips in 1978. Garfield is famous for his love of lasagna and hate for Mondays Known for his attitude, Garfield embodies the ultimate couch potato, often lounging around and delivering cutting remarks about life and those around him. Over the years, the character has become a cultural icon, appearing in television shows, movies, and countless merchandise, continuing to amuse audiences of all ages with his relatable humor and timeless charm.
